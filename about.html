About Us

Young, focused, and growing in India's maritime services sector.

Ekdanta Shipping Services Pvt. Ltd. supports ship owners, managers, and principals with dependable crew manning and marine coordination services.

Company Profile

Established in 2017 with crew management at the core.

Welcome to Ekdanta Shipping Services Pvt. Ltd., a trusted maritime recruitment and crew management company established in 2017 and operating under RPSL No. MUM-470. Since our inception, we have been dedicated to providing reliable and professional manpower solutions to the global shipping industry, connecting qualified seafarers with reputable ship owners and managers worldwide.

At Ekdanta Shipping Services, crew management is not just our business—it is the foundation of our commitment to the maritime sector. Through our extensive network, industry expertise, and dedicated recruitment team, we deliver efficient crew sourcing, documentation support, travel coordination, and complete manpower management services while ensuring full compliance with international maritime regulations.

Our success is built on the quality of our people and the trust of our clients. We carefully select and deploy competent officers, engineers, and ratings who meet the highest standards of professionalism, safety, and performance. By maintaining strong relationships with shipping companies and maritime professionals, we continue to support safe vessel operations and successful seafaring careers across the globe.

Over the years, we have remained committed to integrity, transparency, and service excellence. We are grateful to our valued clients, business partners, and dedicated seafarers whose continued trust and support have contributed to our growth and success. As we look ahead, we remain focused on creating opportunities, delivering dependable maritime manpower solutions, and building long-term value for the global shipping community.
