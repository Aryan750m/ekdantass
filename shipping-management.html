Shipping Management

Reliable and compliant crew manning and ship management solutions.

Ekdanta Shipping Services Pvt. Ltd. is a trusted, RPSL 470 and MLC approved company providing disciplined and qualified manpower for maritime operations worldwide.

Crew & Ship Support

Comprehensive crew planning, sourcing, and logistics support.

We work closely with ship owners, managers, and maritime principals to deliver customized crew planning and coordination. Our structured selection processes ensure that only trained, certified, and experienced seafarers are placed onboard.

Our operational management includes comprehensive certificate verification, pre-joining medical screen coordination, flag state document processing, and travel arrangement support. We ensure seamless handovers to minimize vessel downtime in port.

  • RPSL 470 certified crew manning.
  • Full compliance with MLC and flag state regulations.
  • Continuous support for crew welfare and administrative planning.
